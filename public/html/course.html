• Springhill Hobbit Disc Golf Course
  • Mobile, AL
  • Long and open, with some thick brush along the fairways. Some holes border the creek and 6 holes with elevation.
  • I-65 to Springhill Ave. Go east 1 mile to a left on Park Ave. Go to the end and park on right. 1st basket is at the end of the street. 1st tee to the right at top of the hill. Stones mark tees.
